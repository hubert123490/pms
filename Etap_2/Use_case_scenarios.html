Subject: Car repair shop
Use Case: Make an appointment
Language: English
Preconditions:
  • The user must be logged in to the system.
Postconditions:
  • An appointment
Actors:
  • Client
Main flow:
  1. The customer selects the option "Make an appointment"
  2. The system displays the visit appointment form
  3. The customer completes the form data
  4. The customer approves the form
  5. The system verifies the correctness of the data
  6. The system adds a new visit
Alternate flow:
  • 5 The date of the visit was incorrect GOTO 2
  • 5 Incorrect car VIN number entered GOTO 2
  • 5 Incorrect car registration provided GOTO 2
